About Me

I'm a dedicated Computer Science student at the University of Virginia with a passion for leveraging technology to create meaningful impact. My academic journey is complemented by my experience as a varsity/club soccer player and my role as the founder of Play-It-Forward, a nonprofit organization dedicated to making soccer accessible through equipment donations.

I'm particularly interested in the intersection of technology and social good, with a focus on building sustainable and equitable solutions. My approach combines technical expertise with a deep appreciation for historical context to create innovative solutions to modern challenges.
